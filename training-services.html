Training Services

PTSK9 offer a variety of courses for individuals with their dog companion to NGOs, private security companys or government law enforcement agencies to which the following courses are all available

Puppy imprinting

 

5 - 8 weeks

For those that want to be sure to get the best results as their dog matures. Puppy imprinting establishes the neccassary foundation for later security and protection dog training. Necessary to create a fearless, stable and confident dog.

  • Build strong nerves
  • Begin light bite development for those interested in future protection.
  • Ehance the puppies prey drive.
  • Enhance human awareness and environmental desensitization.
  • Trained to bark on command

OBEDIENCE TRAINING

level 1

5 weeks

This training is for those who desire a confident dog with stable nerves that is well behaved and mostly obedient.

  • Leash training
  • Come
  • Sit
  • Down
  • Bed
  • Stay

OBEDIENCE TRAINING

level 2

5 weeks

Level 2 training is an enhancement of level 1, level 2 gives you off leash control and more command over your dog, this will not however maximize your protection but will make your dog easier to handle in both private and public settings such as when making veterinary visits or going to parks.

  • Heel
  • H'up
  • Climb
  • Leave
  • Eat
  • Additional commands maybe added

OBEDIENCE TRAINING

level 3

5 weeks

Level 3 builds on all the previous levels. Level 3 however more focuses on 'field work,' where your dog is taught to work at a distance with both old and new commands. Mostly off-leash, the dog will obey all commands in all public settings, stop barking when commanded, retrieve and search for objects on land and in water.

  • Quiet
  • Find
  • Bark
  • Bring
  • Swim

PERSONAL PROTECTION dog training

level 1

5 - 8 weeks

Deterrent guard dog.

On command the dog is trained to bark aggressively showing teeth and displaying other intimidating behaviour towards any person directed to. this behaviour is stopped simply by a one word command. The protection dog is trained to be calm and friendly with all people unless commanded otherwise.

  • Trained to deter and threaten on command.
  • Trained to cease its aggression and threatening behaviour on command
  • Trained to fearlessly stand its ground against armed assailants or multiple attackers.

PERSONAL PROTECTION dog training

level 2

5 - 8 weeks

The “Level 2” K-9 is a skilled dog trained in Off-leash Obedience and On-leash Personal Protection. in addition to the level 1 protection dog the level 2 dog will go the extra mile on command of its owner.

  • Trained using real life scenarios.
  • Trained to bite and fight effectively in defense of his owner and family against armed and multiple attackers.
  • Trained to release it bite and cease fighting on command returning to the side of its owner once Heel command is given.

FAMILY PROTECTION dog training

level 3

5 - 8 weeks

Off leash Protection Companion. The “Level 3” K-9 is a more skilled protector that has all the training of the “Level 2” dog. in addition to that, when commanded the level 3 dog will pursue and catch a fleeing assailent, thief or robber, stopping a bad man from escaping and bringing him down to the ground.

  • Trained to pursue and capture a fleeing person or stop a threat from a distance upon your command.
