About us

PTS K9 is a family owned business that began 20 years ago with the training and selling of dogs for protection and guarding by Sharif Ibrahim Kinteh. Since then with the development and expansion of the company and numerous highly trained dogs owned by ourselves and our partners we have incorporated many other K9 security based services, We still continue to train dogs for clients both private and government and offer a wide range of K9 security training for both dogs and handlers. Founded in the UK, PTS-K9 has supplied worldclass security dogs to families, military, law enforcement agencies & security firms internationally.

PTS K9 is committed to developing and providing a portion of the worlds best ever trained dogs to serve and protect humanity. We are one of the worlds first muslim K9 security companys and use our beliefs religious teachings and principles to govern and guide us in our work and methodology of breeding, handling, rearing, training and supplying dogs to people from all corners of the world.

From home protection to canines screening for explosive materials or controlling crowds, our highly trained dogs play an important role along with our certified handlers and trainers who remain on duty and on call to come out and deliver to you a worldclass canine or K9 security service worldwide.

Experience the legendary and true security benefit of PTS K9.

Our current base and international training facility is located at The smiling coast of West africa in The Gambia. visitors are welcome on appointment. Contact us for more info.

Our mission

To be the avant-garde in establishing safety and security through providing elite dogs, handlers and specialist training to The Middle East, Africa and The World
